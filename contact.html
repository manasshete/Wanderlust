Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a custom travel itinerary?

Creating a custom itinerary is easy with Wanderlust! Simply navigate to our "Plan Your Trip" page, enter your destination and travel dates, and follow the step-by-step guide to customize your perfect trip. You can add activities, accommodations, and transportation options based on your preferences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. For certain destinations, we also offer payment plans to help you budget for your dream vacation.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, most bookings can be modified or canceled. Cancellation policies vary depending on the specific services booked and how far in advance you cancel. We recommend reviewing the cancellation policy for each component of your trip during the booking process. For assistance with modifications or cancellations, please contact our customer support team.

How do I sell my travel itinerary on Wanderlust?

To sell your travel itinerary, first create and save it using our trip planner. Then, navigate to the "Share/Sell" tab, set your price, write a compelling description, and select what's included in your package. Once submitted, our team will review your itinerary before listing it in our marketplace. You'll earn a commission each time someone purchases your itinerary!

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options through our trusted partners. We highly recommend purchasing travel insurance for all trips to protect your investment against unexpected events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. You can add insurance during the checkout process.
